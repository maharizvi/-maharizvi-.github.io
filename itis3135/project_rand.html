Project Assignment 1

Overview

  • The application for this project will be for a small business for photography called Maha's Photos. This business is owned and operated by me and my brother, Yousuf Rizvi. For the model of the project, I will be developing the website, and my brother will hold the role of the client. The business currently solely relies on its Instagram page to display a photography portfolio and communicate with potential clients. While Instagram works well to display photos, the client is looking for a website to display different packages for photography options and have all their information listed to make it easy for potential photography clients to locate.
  • The intended users of the website include the client, potential photography clients that are interested in browsing through different photo packages, and current photography clients that can use the website as a reference to go back to find information about the business.
  • To begin with, the contents of the website will be aimed to include: a list of all the photography packages the client offers, prices, descriptions of what is included in each package, images that reflect each package, and a contact form for potential clients to input their information if they are interested in booking a session.

Page Design

Home Page

  1. Name of the page: Home
  2. Purpose of the page: As any home page, this page aims to attract potential clients to explore the contents of the website and have a central point of navigation.
  3. Audience/Users of page: All potential and current clients seeking more information on Maha's Photos will be the intended audience for this page.
  4. Content: Brief introduction, gallery of photos taken by the business, navigation.
  5. Users will not be entering data on this page.
  6. The home page does not require validations.
  7. The home page will include a button for the photo gallery and could potentially also include hyperlinks but no drop down menu.
  8. For the home page, the user has the ability to engage on the arrow mark button in the photo gallery in which the action will either move forward in the slideshow or back depending on the arrow. The hyperlinks that will potentially be included will link to other places for the user to explore.
  9. None

About

  1. Name of the page: About Maha's Photos!
  2. Purpose of the page: This page aims to give users a brief background on the history of the business and the mission behind their photography.
  3. Audience/Users of page: All current and potential photography clients.
  4. Content: History on Maha's Photos began with descriptions of Maha and Yousuf, the owners
  5. No text box prompt.
  6. No validations.
  7. The page will not include buttons, hyperlinks or drop down menu.
  8. This page is a read-me.
  9. None

Contact

  1. Name of the page: Contact Information
  2. Purpose of the page: To give potential and current clients contact information so they can reach the photographer.
  3. Audience/Users of page: Current and potential clients looking to contact the photographer.
  4. Content: Photographer Instagram, Facebook, email, and phone number
  5. No text boxes.
  6. No validations.
  7. Hyperlinks to social media.
  8. Actions only with hyperlink to socials.
  9. No

Events

  1. Name of the page: Photo Packages
  2. Purpose of the page: To give potential clients a place with all of Maha's Photos photography packages, prices, and other information.
  3. Audience/Users of page: Potential clients wanting to choose which photography package they would like to book for.
  4. Content: Each package with its name, session time, price, and what is included in each. Each package will contain an image to give an example.
  5. No text box prompt.
  6. No validations.
  7. No buttons, hyperlinks or drop down menu.
  8. Scrolling actions
  9. None

Book Your Session!

  1. Name of the page: Book Your Session!
  2. Purpose of the page: To allow potential clients to put in a request to book a session by entering their desired date and time and contact information.
  3. Audience/Users of page: Potential photography clients that are ready to book their session.
  4. Content: Enter name, date and time requested, type of session package, and contact
  5. Text box for content above
  6. Validations are to be determined for the form.
  7. No hyperlinks, button for the form.
  8. Action item will submit the form with the required info.
  9. None

Dynamic Functionality

The dynamic functionality within this site will fall most heavily within the page for clients to book their session. This page requires a form functionality for potential clients to input their information and select which type of package they are booking for their session.
