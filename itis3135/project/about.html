About Us!

The Maha's Photos team consists of Maha Rizvi, the photographer, and her brother, Yousuf Rizvi, the editing and retouching expert. This brother-sister business began in 2018 when Maha began to take her hobby on a venture as a small business. Yousuf came to learn more about photo retouching and editing to help Maha focus on her photoshoots. With workshops and courses to train, both siblings perfected their craft and love to work together to bring you the best photos from your session!
